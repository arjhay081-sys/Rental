About Bobby's Motorbike Rental

Your Trusted Partner for Island Adventures

Our Story

Bobby's Motorbike Rental was founded with a simple mission: to provide visitors and locals alike with reliable, affordable, and safe transportation to explore the beautiful Bantayan Island. What started as a small venture has grown into one of the most trusted motorbike rental services in Santa Fe.

Located in the heart of Talisay, Santa Fe, we pride ourselves on offering well-maintained scooters and exceptional customer service. Whether you're a tourist looking to discover hidden beaches or a local needing convenient transportation, we're here to help you navigate Bantayan Island with ease and confidence.

Meet the Owner

Rogelito G. Cabrera

Owner & Barangay Captain of Talisay

Rogelito G. Cabrera is not only the proud owner of Bobby's Motorbike Rental but also the dedicated Barangay Captain of Talisay, Santa Fe, Cebu. His deep commitment to serving the community extends beyond his public service role into his business, where he ensures every customer receives honest, reliable, and trustworthy service.

With years of experience in both community leadership and the tourism industry, Rogelito understands what visitors need to have a safe and memorable experience exploring Bantayan Island. His reputation as a community leader guarantees that you're dealing with a business built on integrity and trust.

Our Fleet

We maintain a fleet of 15 premium scooters, all regularly serviced and safety-checked to ensure your riding experience is smooth and worry-free. Our collection includes popular models perfect for island exploration:

  • Honda Click - Reliable, fuel-efficient, great for island cruising
  • Honda Beat - Lightweight and easy to handle, perfect for all riders
  • Yamaha Fazzio - Comfortable and stylish for longer rides

Every rental includes a complimentary helmet and 24/7 roadside assistance for your peace of mind.
